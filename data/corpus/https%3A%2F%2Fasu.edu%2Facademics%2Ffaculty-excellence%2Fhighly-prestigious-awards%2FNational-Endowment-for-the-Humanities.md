# National Endowment for the Humanities

The National Endowment for the Humanities (NEH) Faculty Research Awards and Fellowships are among those recognized as highly prestigious by the National Academies. The awards listed below exclude small grants for travel (13), consultation (3) and a conference, younger scholars (4) and small grants to libraries (1). We have only included awards made to ASU faculty, while they were with the university.

For more information: https://www.neh.gov
