# Loan Forms

Use this eForm to request additional Federal Direct Unsubsidized Loan funds **only** if your parent was denied a Federal Direct Parent PLUS Loan.

## Instructions

Complete and submit online.

- Click on the eForm below and follow the instructions provided.
- Submit the eForm.

If this item is listed under your **Financing Tasks** in My ASU, your financial aid disbursement will be placed on hold until your parent(s) provide the information requested.

## Why this is needed:

Your parent checked **Yes** to the defaulted loan question on the Federal Direct PLUS Loan application. In order to continue processing your Federal Direct PLUS loan, your parent will need to complete the Parent PLUS default form.

## Purpose

This eForm is used to reduce or cancel an accepted and/or a disbursed Parent PLUS loan. You (the student) will need to submit a request that generates a MyASU Task for your parent to submit the Parent PLUS Loan Action Form.

This eForm should only be submitted by the student. If you are a parent please have your student submit this eForm and monitor your MyASU for the Parent PLUS Loan Action Form Finance Task, which can be submitted to reduce or cancel the Parent PLUS Loan you accepted.

Submit the eForm.

## Why this form is needed

This eForm is used if your parent is ineligible or unable to apply for a Federal Direct Parent PLUS loan due to a special circumstance. You will need to submit a request that generates a MyASU Task for your parent to submit the Parent PLUS Special Circumstance Form and upload the necessary documentation.

Financial Aid and Scholarship Services has determined that you have received more than the total allowable loan limits in Direct Student loans per federal government rules. This means that you are currently ineligible for any type of federal financial aid until you resolve the overage.
