# Student loan repayment

## Manage student loan repayment

If you're a former ASU student and still have student loans, you're not alone — and you don’t have to navigate repayment on your own. Arizona State University is here to ensure you have the information and tools you need to manage your loans effectively and avoid unnecessary stress.

Keep in mind that federal student loans enter repayment **six months after you graduate, leave school or drop below half-time enrollment**. This six-month grace period gives you time to prepare before payments begin.
