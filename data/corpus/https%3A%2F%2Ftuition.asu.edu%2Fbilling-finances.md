# Billing and student finances

## How to manage your account

Billing statements are available online and are not mailed. To view and pay your bills, go to My ASU and select the Finances tab. Here you may review your charges and history, make a payment, and authorize payers.

### Watch video: Navigating MyASU Finances

## How to grant guest access to My ASU

To add parent or guardian access to your billing and finances so they can complete tasks related to your financial aid, complete the guest add form. You need to grant them access because your student account, billing and personally identifiable information are protected by a federal regulation, FERPA, and ASU cannot disclose it without your consent. Find more information about FERPA.

## The ASU payment plan

The ASU payment plan provides students with the option to budget the payment of certain eligible university charges over several months within the semester. **Eligible charges include tuition, housing, food plan, parking permits and health insurance.**

## Paying your bill


Most students use a combination of funding sources to pay for college, including:

- Financial aid.
- Credit and debit cards.
- eChecks.
- Mail-in check.
- Third-party sponsorships.

For information on how to submit payments, visit the payment options page.

## Common payment issues

Late payments and returned checks will result in late fees. If you are having trouble paying, make sure to contact Financial Aid and Scholarship Services to learn about your options.
