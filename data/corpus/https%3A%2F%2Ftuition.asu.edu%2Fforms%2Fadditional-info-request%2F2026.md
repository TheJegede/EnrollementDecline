# 2025-2026 Additional Information Request

Please refer to your ASU email for details regarding what to attach to the eForm. If this item is listed under your **Financing Tasks** in My ASU, your financial aid disbursement will be placed on hold until you provide the information.

## Why this is needed:

After evaluating your financial aid file, it was determined that additional information is necessary.

## Instructions

- Please click on the eForm below and follow the instructions provided.
- Upload the documentation listed in the email sent to your ASU email account.
- Submit the eForm.

You can view your document status on My ASU’s **Finances **tab. Please allow two to three business days after submitting your eForm for our receipt to show in your **Financing Tasks**.
