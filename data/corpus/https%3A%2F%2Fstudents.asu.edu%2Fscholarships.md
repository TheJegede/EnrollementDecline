## Scholarship Universe frequently asked questions

Scholarship Universe is a powerful tool that helps you maximize your scholarship opportunities. By creating an academic profile, you can connect with internal and external scholarships that match your criteria.


Internal scholarships are awarded by ASU, whereas external scholarships come from outside sources. All external scholarships listed in Scholarship Universe are vetted.


Completing your profile ensures that you’ll be matched with scholarships tailored to your background. ASU departments and external groups will use this platform to post future scholarship opportunities, so your profile will remain useful throughout your academic experience.


Yes, you must be an admitted or current ASU student. Use your ASURITE User ID and password to log in to Scholarship Universe and start exploring scholarships.


- Visit Scholarship Universe and log in with your ASURITE User ID and password.
- Complete your student profile to be matched with scholarships.
- Use the “Scholarships” tab to find and apply for opportunities.
- Regularly check for new matches and track your application status.

Yes, there are multiple ways to search for scholarships in Scholarship Universe:

- Search function: Use the Search bar at the top-right corner by your name to search by keywords or tags.
- Scholarships tab: Select the Scholarships tab and use the Sort and Filter options in the upper-right corner to search within categories, such as Matches and Pinned, etc.
- Filter option: Narrow your search by organization, academic department or other criteria using the available filters.
