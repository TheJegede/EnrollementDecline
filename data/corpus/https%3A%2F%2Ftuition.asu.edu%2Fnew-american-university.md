# New American University Scholar

## To be a New American University Scholar means more than assistance with financing your education, it includes a unique opportunity to begin making a difference while you earn your degree.

## First-year Merit Scholarship Estimator

Use our First-year Merit Scholarship Estimator to find out if you qualify to become a New American University Scholar. Just enter your GPA, competencies and ACT/SAT score. Your scholarship award will be based upon your official academic credentials at the time of your admission to ASU. Be sure to request that your test scores be sent directly to ASU from the testing service.


## Apply for Admission

ASU considers students for merit-based scholarships once they have been admitted to ASU. If you have not yet applied to ASU, submit an application for undergraduate admission and accompanying materials (official transcripts, test scores and application fee). Note that there are different priority dates for each scholarship.

## Scholarship Renewal Criteria

See the Scholarship Renewal Guide for information about maintaining scholarships. The Barack Obama Scholars program and College Attainment Grant have separate renewal standards.
