## Visit ASU in person

We’re excited to welcome you! ASU offers two types of campus visit events for you and your family to explore what it’s like to be a Sun Devil: Experience ASU and Sun Devil Day.


### What to expect when you visit

Before coming to ASU, check out our tips on navigating each campus. Our “What to Expect” pages contain parking, location, check-in and important updates for your campus visit, whether that be Experience ASU, Sun Devil Day or Transfer Day. Important note: We require all visitors to register before arriving on campus. For more information on how to register, select the “On-campus visits and events” section above.

## Visit ASU virtually

Can’t get to ASU? You can still explore ASU from where you are.

## ASU campuses in metro Phoenix

### ASU California Center

Steps from the Fashion District and at the center of the nation’s film and television industry, the ASU California Center in Los Angeles offers professional and academic opportunities for students. ASU students become prepared for careers in film, fashion, virtual reality, law, government, public service, journalism, media and the corporate sector.

### ASU London

ASU’s newest location, ASU London, is located in Islington, central London at the City St. George’s Clerkenwell campus. Surrounded by some of central London’s most famous landmarks and other universities that give the area an energetic college vibe, ASU London offers three-year U.K. bachelor’s degrees in computer science, engineering and business management. Students then head to the U.S. to complete their master’s degree at ASU.
