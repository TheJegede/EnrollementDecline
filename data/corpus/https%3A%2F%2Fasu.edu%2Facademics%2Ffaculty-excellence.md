## Recognizing world-class faculty

The Arizona State University faculty is at the forefront nationally in advancing research and discovery. Our more than 5,600 faculty members inspire new ways of thinking, innovating and solving problems socially, culturally and economically in our region and in the international community.

We aspire to create an accessible academic experience and attract faculty who are not bound by traditional disciplinary distinctions, but instead embrace an inclusive, collaborative and entrepreneurial environment defined by excellence and impact. This commitment is reflected in the achievements of ASU faculty, who have received 58 notable awards, alongside many others who hold highly prestigious awards or distinguished memberships and honors.
