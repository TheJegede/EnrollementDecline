# Source: https://tuition.asu.edu/forms/private-loan-self-certification

**Private education loans** | - Follow the instructions included on the form at the bottom of this page.
- In order to complete Section 2 of the form, you will need to access your ASU cost of attendance and estimated financial assistance by following these instructions:
- Click here to view your financial aid and select the applicable aid year for your loan.
- Click on the
**Financial Aid Summary** link to the left of the **Accept/Decline Awards** button. - Write the amount listed under "Estimated Financial Aid Budget" as your cost of attendance on Section 2, Item A of the form.
- Write the amount listed under "Total Aid" as your estimated financial assistance on Section 2, Item B of the form.
- Complete Section 2, Item C using the formula on the form.
- If you have any questions, please contact our office.
**Submit this form to your lender; do not submit this form to ASU Financial Aid and Scholarship Services.**
|
