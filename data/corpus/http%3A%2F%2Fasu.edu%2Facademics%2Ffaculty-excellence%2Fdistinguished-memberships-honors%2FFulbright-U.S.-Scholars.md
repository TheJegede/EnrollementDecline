# Fulbright U.S. Scholars

Sponsored by the U.S. Department of State's, Bureau of Educational and Cultural Affairs, the Fulbright Program provides funding for students, scholars, teachers and professionals to undertake graduate study, advanced research, university teaching, and teaching in elementary and secondary schools abroad.

Arizona State University is recognized as one of the top producers of faculty Fulbright U.S Scholars. Please contact [email protected] for more information or to apply for the U.S. Scholar Program. If you are a student, information is available from Barrett, The Honors College at Fulbright at ASU.
