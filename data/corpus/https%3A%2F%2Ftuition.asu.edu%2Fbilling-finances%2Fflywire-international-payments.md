# International payments

Arizona State University has partnered with Flywire to offer a streamlined and cost effective payment method to send international payments. Flywire offers competitive exchange rates and a secure portal for processing payments to ASU. Create a Flywire login to make payments and review your payment activity and history.

To make an international payment to one of the following ASU departments, please click on the link below to take you to the appropriate web page to begin your process.

ASU Tuition and Fees for Students


To make payments to your ASU student account through My ASU under the **Finances** tab.

ASU Tuition and Fees for Authorized Payers


**Students do not use this option, see "ASU Tuition and Fees for Students" above. **Third Party Agencies are encouraged to use this link only after recieving an official invoice for their sponsored student from ASU Student Business Services. For more details, see third-party sponsorships for additional information.

ASU Third Party Sponsor Payment


To pay an ASU departmental invoice for services received or goods purchased by international payment. For vendors only.

ASU Departmental Vendor Payment


For international students to complete and pay your ASU undergraduate admissions application fee.

ASU Undergraduate Admissions Application Fee


International students to complete and pay for Campus Immersion, Online Immersion, ASU-USA Pathways and English for Undergraduate or Graduate Admission and Achievement application fees.

ASU Global Launch Application Fee


To register and pay for ASU continuing education courses.
